FormIt::Utils::ModelStatSummary Struct Reference

Description

A summary of all the model stats.

Note that for bodies, faces, edges, and vertices, the count is how many total there are in the model, not the unique count. For instance, if you had a group with a single cube instanced 5 times, then you would have 30 faces, not 6. Materials, textures, and images are counted as unique instances.
